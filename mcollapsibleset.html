MCollapsibleSet allows you to create a group of Panels. When one of the headers of the Panels in the group is clicked, the other ones get closed.

  • Control the Theme and color variation of a MCollapsibleSet with the MobileTheme component associated to it.
  • Use Panels to create the different panels's headers 
  • ActiveLayer indicates the name of the panel  that is open.
  • When a Panel is expanded, onExpand and onCollapse events are fired
