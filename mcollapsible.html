This Panel was hidden because its isCollapsed property was set to true.

As you can see MCollapsible controls can contain other MCollapsible controls.

MCollapsible is a container with a Header that can show/hide its content. 

  • Control the Theme and color variation of a group of MCollapsible with the MobileTheme component associated to them.
  • Use Caption to change the text on the header
  • With isCollapsed you can indicate if the container's content will be hidden/shown
